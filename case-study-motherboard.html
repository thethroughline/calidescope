PERFORM · MOTHERBOARD · IPG MEDIABRANDS
A job that had failed three times before.
2015–2017
ALSO READS AS PROMISE
SITUATION
A global advertiser's pitch question exposed the gap: there was no way to see media planning work across markets, keep it consistent, or let markets collaborate on it.
Getting 30,000 people in 130 markets onto one workflow had been tried three times before. It had failed three times before.
TASK
Build the platform that made cross-market planning visible, consistent, and collaborative — and prove it under live pitch conditions.
ACTION
Built Motherboard as a rapid alpha for the Unilever pitch, proved it again on the Coca-Cola pitch — won on the strength of it — then scaled it into a robust enterprise build with Google sponsorship, integrating Google's media, measurement, and creative APIs into the pan-agency stack.
RESULT
Won
THE COCA-COLA PITCH
$7M/yr
GOOGLE PARTNERSHIP
130
