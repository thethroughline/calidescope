PERFORM · WALT DISNEY INTERNET GROUP
Personalization, before it had a name.
1999–2001
SITUATION
Disney's internet group ran the company's entire online portfolio — Disney Online, Go.com, Parks, and eCommerce — at the height of the first internet boom.
The data had to actually move product — park tickets, merchandise, subscriptions — across four very different businesses, inside one of the most demanding brand organizations in the world.
TASK
Deploy enterprise CRM across the portfolio and make personalization work in production.
ACTION
Deployed e.piphany — one of the era's defining CRM platforms — across the digital properties, and built statistical buyer-behavior models and personalization strategies serving each business.
