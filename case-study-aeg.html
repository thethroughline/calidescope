PERFORM · AEG
A portfolio of venues with no shared system.
2003–2007
SITUATION
STAPLES Center and its portfolio of teams and events had no shared CRM or ticketing data connecting any of it.
The biggest test was The O2 in London — a $650M venue built inside the former Millennium Dome — which needed the same systems built from scratch, on a bigger stage, before it could open.
TASK
Build the CRM and ticketing infrastructure to make an entire venue portfolio addressable, and get The O2 open on time.
ACTION
Built the CRM and interactive ticketing systems for STAPLES Center, the LA Kings, LA Galaxy, and Coachella, then led the same build for The O2 — including contactless ticketing and artist pre-sale infrastructure that was ahead of its time in 2007.
RESULT
$50M
TICKET SALES ENABLED
$650M
