PROMISE · MARKETSHARE PARTNERS · LONDON
Proving search drove store sales — through the Great Recession.
2007–2010
ALSO READS AS PROOF
SITUATION
MarketShare sent one strategist to London to build its international business, arriving with a single client and a plan to recruit a sales director and staff up.
The global financial crisis hit immediately after, and the hiring budget was withdrawn — building the business became a one-person job.
TASK
Prove a claim nobody had proven before: that Google searches were driving purchases in physical stores — the ROPO effect, research online, purchase offline — and turn that proof into an international business.
ACTION
Used a dataset only Google had — one that connected online research to offline purchase — to build ROPO case studies offline retailers couldn't argue with, then parlayed them into work for Sony, Auchan, Carrefour, and Disney across EMEA and Asia-Pacific, eventually hiring and managing a team of eight.
RESULT
$2M

~$5M
