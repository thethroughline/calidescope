PROOF · HAVAS · CONVERGED
Five CEOs, each with their own audience platform. No reason to agree on one.
2018–2020
SITUATION
Havas's markets each had their own data roadmap and their own committed local supplier.
Building one global data platform meant convincing five market CEOs — UK, France, Germany, Spain, and the Netherlands — to give up their individual roadmaps, with no obvious reason for any of them to defer to the others.
TASK
Design a single global data and innovation platform that five independent markets would actually agree to join.
ACTION
Built Converged — a platform that worked with each market's existing supplier instead of forcing one common vendor, unifying them into one system for planning, buying, and measurement — then staffed a 20+ person global team across five cities in eighteen months.
