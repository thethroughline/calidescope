PERFORM · HOUSE OF BLUES
A merger, and no shared customer record.
2001–2002
ALSO READS AS PROOF
SITUATION
House of Blues had just merged with Universal Concerts into eight clubs and fifteen amphitheaters.
There was no unified way of knowing who its customers were across any of them.
TASK
Build a database-marketing and membership system that turned anonymous concertgoers into one addressable audience across the merged company.
ACTION
Built online ticket pre-sale programs and offline data collection at venues, stood up membership infrastructure, and negotiated partnerships with MSN, AOL, and Yahoo Music to put House of Blues content in front of their audiences.
RESULT
$25M
IN SALES GENERATED
23
VENUES UNIFIED
3
