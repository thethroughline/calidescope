PROOF · AMERICAN EXPRESS · UM WORLDWIDE
One review away from losing the account.
2022–2023
SITUATION
An enterprise media account — one of the portfolio's largest — was approaching a formal client review, serious enough that an outside relationship consultancy had already been brought in.
Analytics and reporting were a specific point of friction: media, analytics, creative, and data were each producing separate reports that didn't agree, leaving the client to reconcile the differences themselves.
TASK
Fix the analytics and reporting function before the review — replace five conflicting reports with one story the client didn't have to reconcile.
ACTION
Introduced a weekly cadence where every discipline contributed one piece to a single shared client narrative — one story, assembled from everyone's ingredients, instead of five decks.
RESULT
~3 mo.
TIMELINE
Avoided
