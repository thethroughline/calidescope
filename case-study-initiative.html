PROOF · INITIATIVE
Five analytics teams — some paid, some given away.
2010–2017
ALSO READS AS PERFORM
SITUATION
Initiative's analytics and research capability was split across five departments: digital analytics, social analytics, marketing-mix modeling, consumer research, and media research.
Some of the work was getting paid for; the rest was being given away — and work that’s given away has no reason to get better.
TASK
Band the five departments into one practice that gets paid for its work — because paid work has to be sold well and done well, and each makes the other better.
ACTION
Bundled the five departments for pricing protection, packaged the work so it could be sold — starting small and growing — and built the Motherboard, the workflow tool Google paid for, connecting the process end to end and the teams to each other.
